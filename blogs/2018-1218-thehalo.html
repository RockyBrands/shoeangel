The Halo

As a healthcare professional you have had a profound impact on the lives of many people. Your selfless dedication results in long hours on your feet working for the well-being of everyone and everything in your care.

Our mission here at Shoe Angel is to put you first; to look out for you, our guardian angels in the healthcare industry, and make you feel good so that you can continue to do good.

Shoe Angel partners with healthcare facilities to implement footwear benefit programs free of charge, which then allows us to provide premier work and lifestyle shoes and boots at an amazing discount to associates of all types and titles. We are also proud to give 5% of every Shoe Angel purchase to charity, benefiting our partners’ communities through donations to their foundation, auxiliary, or other charities of choice.

Shoe Angel launched in January 2018 and is constantly growing. We offer over 20 brands and over 2000 styles of footwear to keep you comfortable, healthy, and safe through all the challenges of your day. Included are brands that are known and loved by clinical staff, such as Dansko, Nursemates, and KLOGS; slip-resistant athletic styles from brands such as Fila, Reebok, and Skechers, along with amazing protective toe and lifestyle brands like Rocky, Georgia Boot, and Durango. There’s something for everyone to choose from, with more brands and styles always being added.
