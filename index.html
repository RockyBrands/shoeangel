What is Shoe Angel??

test

Shoe Angel provides a free managed safety footwear program for the healthcare industry.

Our program helps with safety in the workplace by giving the tools that all healthcare practitioners need to provide appropriate footwear. Shoe Angel reduces your workplace accident liability by providing a standard program which gives you the tools required to manage and analyze such an important program.

Brands

Shoe Angel provides all the brands you know and love from Skechers to Nurse Mates to Nautilus. We've got hundreds of styles to choose from, which allows for a huge selection at the best prices online. By utilizing Shoe Angel at your workplace, we pass the savings on to you!

FAQ

Who should use Shoe Angel?
All hospitality companies that need to ensure their employees are protected and properly outfitted.

How much does it cost?
Shoe Angel is a free program. The only cost is that of the footwear, which are discounted when you setup an account. Get started today!

Does Shoe Angel sell other hospitality-related products?
While our primary focus is protective footwear, we offer other products as well and are always increasing our product offering.

What if the shoes don't fit?
We recognize this common concern and offer free shipping and returns on everything. No minimums.

What else does Shoe Angel provide?
Our program offers many different payment integrations, on-site ordering kiosks, employee vouchers, subsidy & payroll deduction, multi-employee ordering, and so much more. Contact us to find out more about the program.
